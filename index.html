Profile Summary

Dr. Oladapo Olalekan Adeduro is a cybersecurity researcher and academic at McPherson University, Nigeria. His research focuses on cloud data security, cryptographic algorithms, and machine learning. He has published in peer-reviewed journals and conferences and supervises postgraduate research in computing.
